Reinventing India

Reinventing India captures the fascinating evolution of India’s Science, Technology and Innovation (STI) landscape through some of Dr.Mashelkar's most inspiring and insightful lectures and essays. Readers will be unable to resist his contagious optimism about India’s future!
Sahyadri Publications, 2011

 

 

Timeless Inspirator:Reliving Gandhi

In Timeless Inspirator iconic achievers – from sportsmen to scientists – explain how Mahatma Gandhi's principles, wisdom and values are relevant in today’s challenging times. These 45 essays are masterpieces that emanate straight from the heart and bring Bapu’s timeless inspiration alive!
Sakal Publications, 2010
